Timecard Management

Mortenson’s policy is to gather timely and accurate information on all hours worked and non-worked.

Non-Craft Team Member Toolbox

Scroll down to your role and select the link for more information on completing your timecard.

Average Coster

Do you charge all of your time to a project and task?

Then you’re an Average Coster.

Average Costers are non-craft team members whose time is charged to a direct project and task. Most non-worked hours are included with the exception of a sabbatical and holidays. An example includes: Energy Storage-Project Management.

Are you paid a salary? Click here to learn how to complete your timecard.

Are you paid hourly? Click here to learn how to complete your timecard.

Standard Coster

Do you charge some of your time to a project and task?

Then you’re a Standard Coster.

Standard costers are non-craft team members who charge some of their time to a direct project and task and/or indirect projects. They do not charge time not worked (such as PTO) to a project. Examples include: HQ-Data Analysis, Data Center-M-Estimating, Power-Quality, Energy Storage-Design Phase, etc.

No Coster

If you don’t charge any of your time to a project and task,

Then you’re a No Coster.

Examples include: HQ-Group Finance, Engineering, HQ-Talent Development, ITS, etc.
